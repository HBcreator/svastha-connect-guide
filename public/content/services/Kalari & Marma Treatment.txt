**What is Kalaripayattu? The Mother of All Martial Arts**

*Kalaripayattu* is an ancient martial art form from Kerala, South India, renowned for its fluid, graceful movements that emulate the power and agility of animals. However, it is much more than a combat system. It is a holistic discipline designed to achieve perfect control over the body and mind. The training makes the practitioner acutely aware of the body's energy flows and its most vital points—knowledge that is crucial for both fighting and healing.

**Unlocking the Body’s Secrets: An Introduction to Marma Therapy**

The healing side of *Kalaripayattu* is known as *Marma Chikitsa*. The word *Marma* in Sanskrit means a secret or hidden vital point. According to ancient texts, there are <b>108 marma points</b> on the human body, which are critical junctions where flesh, veins, arteries, tendons, bones, and joints meet.

More importantly, these are potent energy hubs where *prana*, or the life force energy, is concentrated. When the flow of *prana* through these points is blocked or stagnant due to injury, stress, or lifestyle issues, it can lead to pain, stiffness, and disease. Marma therapy is the art of clearing these blockages.

### Facilities & Amenities

At Dhathri, every aspect of your stay is thoughtfully designed to support your healing journey in an environment of comfort and authenticity.

**Diverse Accommodation Options**

Rest and recover in a space designed for your comfort, privacy, and healing.

*   Choose from a range of options, from simple, healing-focused rooms to deluxe AC comfort.

*   All rooms feature modern ensuite bathrooms, comfortable beds, and daily housekeeping.

*   Enjoy a clean, peaceful, and well-ventilated space with plenty of natural light.

**Premium Backwater Cottages**

For a truly immersive healing experience, our premium resort cottages offer unparalleled tranquility.

*   Stay in private AC cottages with stunning, uninterrupted views of the Kerala backwaters.

*   Wake up to the sound of birds and practice yoga overlooking the serene waters.

*   The perfect choice for couples or those seeking ultimate privacy and enhanced comfort.

**Authentic Treatment Facilities**

Experience the best of traditional healing, supported by modern standards of hygiene and safety.

*   Heal in comfort in our dedicated therapy rooms, designed for authentic Ayurvedic procedures.

*   Benefit from the purity of our own in-house pharmacy, where your medicines are freshly prepared.

*   Trust in our care, with NABH-accredited hygiene and sterilization protocols in place.

**Wellness & Recreational Facilities**

Your healing extends beyond the treatment room with facilities that nurture your mind, body, and spirit.

*   Harmonize your mind and body in our spacious hall for daily yoga and meditation.

*   Deepen your understanding of your healing with educational walks through our medicinal herbal garden.

*   Accelerate your recovery with our supportive physiotherapy unit, especially for spine and joint care.

**Therapeutic Ayurvedic Dining**

Savor delicious meals where food is treated as the first and most powerful medicine.

*   Enjoy simple, wholesome, and delicious vegetarian meals prepared according to classical Ayurvedic principles.

*   Your meals are customized by our physicians to support your specific treatment plan and balance your *doshas*.

*   Stay hydrated and supported with specially prepared herbal-infused water throughout the day.

**Medical & Safety Infrastructure**

Feel completely secure with our professional medical support and internationally recognized safety standards.

*   Enjoy complete peace of mind with our Ayurvedic physicians available on call 24/7.

*   Our NABH accreditation ensures that every aspect of your care meets rigorous safety protocols.

*   Receive systematic documentation of your treatment journey for your records and any insurance needs.

**Hassle-Free Guest Services**

We handle all the practical details so you can focus completely on your healing journey.

Our center offers the perfect balance of peaceful seclusion and convenient accessibility.

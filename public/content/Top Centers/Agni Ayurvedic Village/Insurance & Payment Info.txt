
### Insurance & Payment Info

Making your path to holistic healing accessible with flexible payment and insurance support.

**Insurance Coverage**
*   Many of our medical treatments may be covered by your Indian health insurance provider.
*   We offer a cashless facility for eligible conditions to make your stay seamless.
*   Our team provides all necessary medical documentation to support your claims process.
*   We advise you to confirm coverage details directly with your insurance provider beforehand.

**Payment Options**
*   We accept all major payment methods, including credit/debit cards, UPI, and bank transfers.
*   Our pricing is transparent, with all costs clearly outlined before your stay begins.
*   Flexible payment options and special pricing are available for corporate and group retreats.
*   Please inquire about any seasonal offers or special package rates when you book.

**For International Patients**
International guests are encouraged to check with their home insurance providers regarding coverage for Ayurvedic treatments. Our administrative team is experienced in providing the necessary medical reports and documentation to support your international insurance claims.

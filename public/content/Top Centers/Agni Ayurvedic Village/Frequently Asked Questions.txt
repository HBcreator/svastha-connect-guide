### Frequently Asked Questions About Agni Ayurvedic Village

**1. What is the minimum duration for wellness programs at Agni Ayurvedic Village?**

Wellness programs can range from weekend detox escapes (3-5 days) to comprehensive week-long retreats (7-10 days) and intensive Panchakarma programs (14-21 days). Minimum stay duration depends on chosen wellness program and individual health goals. Even brief weekend stays provide noticeable relaxation benefits and introduction to Ayurveda, while longer durations of 2-4 weeks offer deeper therapeutic results, substantial health improvements, and lasting wellness transformations.

**2. How far is Agni Ayurvedic Village from Mumbai and how do I reach there?**

Agni is conveniently located just 1.5 to 2 hours drive from Mumbai city center and Mumbai International Airport, situated in Morbe Village near Panvel on Panvel-Tamasi Marg. The resort provides complimentary pickup and drop services from Mumbai Airport and Panvel area. Exact directions and transportation assistance are provided upon booking confirmation, making access remarkably easy for Mumbai residents and international travelers.

**3. Do I need prior experience with Ayurveda to benefit from treatments?**

No prior Ayurvedic experience is necessary. Agni welcomes complete beginners and provides comprehensive initial consultations where doctors explain treatment protocols, answer all questions, and design gentle introduction programs. The compassionate medical team personalizes all treatments according to individual comfort levels, health conditions, previous experience with wellness therapies, and personal preferences, ensuring safe and effective healing journeys for first-time Ayurveda seekers.

**4. What type of food is served and can special dietary requirements be accommodated?**

Agni serves exclusively therapeutic Satvik vegetarian Ayurvedic cuisine prepared by award-winning Chef Jacob using fresh organic ingredients. Three nutritious meals daily plus tea/coffee are included, customized according to individual dosha constitutions and treatment requirements. While the menu is vegetarian by Ayurvedic principle, the kitchen can accommodate food allergies, intolerances, and specific dietary preferences with advance notice. The simple yet delicious food proves healthy eating can be genuinely satisfying.

**5. Is Agni Ayurvedic Village suitable for families with children?**

Absolutely. Agni is family-friendly and welcomes children, offering specialized pediatric Ayurvedic consultations and age-appropriate wellness therapies. The spacious 68-acre property provides safe natural environment for children to explore, swimming pool facilities, and family accommodation options. Some reviewers suggest separate meal options for children transitioning to vegetarian Satvik food. The peaceful atmosphere, caring staff, and engaging activities make it ideal for family wellness retreats where parents receive treatments while children enjoy nature.

**6. What makes Agni different from other Ayurvedic centers?**

Agni's unique advantages include being Maharashtra's largest Ayurveda hospital spread across 68 acres with stunning mountain views and forest ambiance, NABH accreditation ensuring quality standards, convenient 1.5-2 hour proximity to Mumbai unlike distant Kerala resorts, 25+ years of Ayurvedic legacy, experienced medical team led by passionate doctors like Dr. Rajiv and Dr. Bhavya, authentic Kerala-style architecture and treatments, complimentary airport transfers, three daily yoga sessions, award-winning Satvik cuisine by Chef Jacob, and personalized care creating extended family atmosphere rather than impersonal hotel experience.

**7. What should I pack for my stay at Agni Ayurvedic Village?**

Comfortable loose cotton clothing suitable for treatments and Maharashtra climate, personal toiletries, any regular medications with prescriptions, previous medical reports if applicable, swimwear for pool, sunscreen, insect repellent, walking shoes for exploring the vast 68-acre property and forest trails, camera for capturing beautiful natural surroundings, notebook for wellness learning, and most importantly an open mind ready for transformative healing. Agni provides treatment gowns, towels, yoga mats, and all necessary therapy amenities.

**8. Are there any activities besides Ayurvedic treatments?**

Yes, Agni offers comprehensive wellness activities beyond treatments including three daily yoga sessions with experienced instructor Mr. Narendra (morning energizing practice, afternoon gentle stretches, evening relaxation), guided pranayama breathing exercises, meditation sessions, nature walks through 68 acres of forest and medicinal plant gardens, bird watching opportunities, swimming pool enjoyment, evening cultural programs occasionally, wellness education sessions, cooking demonstrations, and plenty of peaceful time for reading, contemplation, and digital detox in the serene natural setting away from urban distractions.

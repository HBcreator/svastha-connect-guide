### Treatment Process & Patient Journey

Your personalized path to wellness, thoughtfully guided every step of the way in our serene riverside sanctuary.

1.  **Initial Consultation & Health Assessment**
    Your healing journey begins with a deep and personal consultation to understand your unique health story and goals.
    *   You will be personally guided by our expert Ayurvedic physician.
    *   Through traditional diagnostics like pulse reading (*Nadi Pariksha*), we uncover your unique mind-body constitution (*Prakriti*).
    *   We deeply listen to understand your health concerns and identify the root cause of any imbalance.

2.  **Personalized Treatment Protocol Design**
    Based on your consultation, our physicians will craft your exclusive wellness blueprint, a detailed roadmap to restore your balance.

    *   It will specify your bespoke schedule of Ayurvedic therapies and any necessary *Panchakarma* procedures.
    *   Your plan also includes a tailored diet, herbal medicines, and lifestyle guidance for holistic results.

3.  **Daily Ayurvedic Treatments & Therapies**
    This is where your deep healing and rejuvenation truly begin, with daily therapies delivered with skill and compassionate care.

5.  **Daily Wellness Activities & Relaxation**
    Your wellness journey extends beyond the treatment room into a world of mindful relaxation and connection with nature.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**1. What is the minimum recommended stay?**

For our wellness and rejuvenation programs, we recommend a minimum stay of 7 days to experience tangible benefits. For our medical treatment programs, a stay of 14 days or more is typically required for deep and effective healing, as advised by our physicians.

**2. What kind of food is served at the centre?**

We serve a delicious and wholesome vegetarian diet based on classical Ayurvedic principles. Your meals are considered a vital part of your treatment and are customized by our physicians to suit your unique *dosha* and health condition.

**3. Should I bring my regular medications with me?**

Yes, please bring any prescribed medications you are currently taking, along with the relevant prescriptions or a letter from your doctor. Our Ayurvedic physicians will review them during your initial consultation and advise you on how to manage them during your stay.

**4. Is Nagarjuna a hospital or a resort?**

We are a fully NABH-accredited Ayurvedic hospital, which means our primary focus is on providing authentic, high-quality medical care in a safe and clinical environment. However, our serene, riverside location and traditional architecture provide the peaceful ambiance of a wellness retreat.

**5. How do I get to the centre?**

We are conveniently located in Kalady, which is near the Cochin International Airport (COK) in Kerala. We are happy to help you coordinate your airport transfers and other transportation arrangements for a seamless and stress-free journey.

**6. What should I pack for my stay?**

We recommend packing light, comfortable cotton clothing suitable for a warm climate and for wearing during yoga sessions. Please also bring any personal toiletries, your medical records, and a good book. We will provide towels and any necessary attire for treatments.

**7. Are the treatments personalized for me?**

Absolutely. There are no one-size-fits-all packages at Nagarjuna. Following your comprehensive initial consultation, our physicians will design a completely unique treatment plan tailored to your specific health needs, goals, and constitution.

**8. Can my family or a companion stay with me?**

Yes, companions are welcome to stay with you. We can arrange accommodation for them, and they can enjoy the serene environment and our healthy Ayurvedic cuisine. Please note that treatments are only provided to those who have undergone a consultation and have a prescribed wellness plan.

**9. Is there internet or Wi-Fi available?**

Limited Wi-Fi is available in designated areas. However, we gently encourage our guests to embrace a digital detox as much as possible, as disconnecting from screens is a powerful way to calm the mind and deepen your healing experience.

**10. Is the centre suitable for elderly patients?**

Yes, we offer specialized geriatric care and are well-equipped to provide for the needs of our senior guests. Our physicians design gentle yet effective programs for age-related conditions, and our compassionate staff are always available to provide support.

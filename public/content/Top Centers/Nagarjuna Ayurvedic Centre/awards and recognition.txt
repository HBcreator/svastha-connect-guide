ISO 9001:2015 Certification (Intertek)

Intertek dwara pradan ki gayi ISO 9001:2015 certification, jo hamare quality management systems, patient care standards aur operational excellence ko validate karti hai.

ISO 9001:2015 Certification (DAS – UK Accredited)

DAS (UKAS-accredited) se prapt ISO 9001 certification, jo international quality benchmarks ke anuroop hamari Ayurvedic services ki consistency aur reliability ko darshati hai.

UKAS Quality Management Recognition

UKAS quality management recognition, jo globally accepted standards ke tahat hamari clinical processes aur administrative controls ki authenticity ko confirm karti hai.

NABH Accreditation

NABH accreditation, jo patient safety, ethical medical practices aur holistic healthcare delivery ke liye hamari commitment ko officially acknowledge karti hai.

GMP – Good Manufacturing Practice Certified

GMP certification, jo is baat ka pramaan hai ki hamare Ayurvedic medicines aur formulations strict quality, hygiene aur safety standards ke tahat taiyar kiye jaate hain.

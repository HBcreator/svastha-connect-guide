### Insurance & Payment Info

Making your path to authentic healing clear and accessible with flexible payment and insurance support.

**Insurance Coverage**

*   As a NABH-accredited hospital, our medical treatments are often eligible for insurance claims.

*   We provide support for cashless facilities for eligible conditions, making your stay seamless.

*   Our dedicated administrative team provides all necessary medical documentation to support your claims.

*   We strongly advise you to confirm coverage details directly with your insurance provider beforehand.

**Payment Options**

*   We accept all major payment methods, including credit/debit cards and bank transfers.

*   Our pricing is completely transparent, with all costs clearly outlined in a personalized quotation.

*   Flexible payment options and special pricing are available for corporate and group retreats.

*   Please inquire about any seasonal offers or special package rates when you make your booking.

**For International Patients**

International guests are encouraged to check with their home insurance providers regarding coverage for Ayurvedic treatments. Our administrative team is experienced in providing the necessary medical reports and documentation to support your international insurance claims.

**How to Reach**

**By Air:** Cochin International Airport is the nearest airport located approximately 90 km from both Dheemahi locations. Airport taxi services and pre-arranged transportation available. Journey takes approximately 2 hours through scenic Kerala countryside.

**By Train:** Kottayam Railway Station is well-connected to major Indian cities including Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai. Dheemahi Village is 16 km from station, Dheemahi Neelimangalam just 3 km. Local taxis, auto-rickshaws readily available. Transportation can be arranged through the center.

**By Road:** Both locations accessible by road from major Kerala cities. Well-connected via National Highway network. Private vehicles, taxis, and state transport buses available. GPS coordinates and detailed directions provided upon booking confirmation.

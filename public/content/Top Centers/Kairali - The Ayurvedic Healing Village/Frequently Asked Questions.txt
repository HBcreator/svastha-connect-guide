### Frequently Asked Questions About Kairali - The Ayurvedic Healing Village

**1. What is the minimum duration for treatment programs at Kairali?**

Minimum recommended stay is 7 days for wellness programs, 14 days for detoxification Panchakarma, and 21-28 days for treating chronic medical conditions. Kairali's proven track record shows 53% symptomatic relief within just 3 days and 87% success rate in chronic disease treatment, but longer durations provide deeper therapeutic transformation and lasting health improvements.

**2. How far is Kairali from major cities and airports?**

Kairali is located in Palakkad district, Kerala. Nearest airport is Coimbatore International Airport (45 km, 1 hour drive). Calicut International Airport is 90 km away. Palakkad Railway Station is 20 km from the property. The serene rural location away from urban chaos provides naturally therapeutic environment essential for healing.

**3. Is the alcohol-free, vegetarian environment mandatory?**

Yes, Kairali maintains strictly alcohol-free, vegetarian, caffeine-free, smoking-free environment as fundamental to authentic Ayurvedic healing. This sattvic atmosphere is not optional but essential therapeutic protocol. All food prepared fresh daily from organic farm without refrigeration, preservatives, or artificial ingredients, embodying true Ayurvedic nutritional principles where food becomes medicine.

Kairali's 116-year legacy since 1908, NABH-certified hospital status, 87% chronic disease success rate, 230,000+ treated patients, world-renowned awards (World Luxury Hotel Awards, National Geographic Top 50), 60-acre Laurie Baker-designed property, 6000 sq ft yoga center, organic farming, complete sattvic environment, and 17 branches across 5 countries distinguish it as authentic healing destination versus commercial wellness resorts.

Absolutely. Kairali specializes in treating elderly patients and serious chronic conditions with gentle, age-appropriate therapies.

### Treatment Process & Patient Journey

Your personalized path to wellness, thoughtfully guided every step of the way.


**Pre-Arrival Consultation**

Your healing journey begins with a thoughtful consultation to ensure we are the perfect fit for your needs.

*   We start with a detailed discussion of your health history and wellness goals.

*   Our medical team reviews your information to provide guidance on the most suitable programs.

*   This sets a clear and honest foundation for a successful and transformative healing journey.

**Arrival Welcome & Deep Diagnosis**

From the moment you arrive, you are enveloped in the tranquility of our village and the warmth of our expert care.

*   Receive a traditional Kerala welcome that immediately puts you at ease in our serene sanctuary.

*   Our experienced Ayurvedic physicians will conduct a thorough medical examination.

*   Through traditional diagnostics like pulse reading (*Nadi Pariksha*), we will identify your unique constitution (*Prakriti*).

**Personalized Treatment Blueprint**

Based on our deep diagnosis, our doctors will design your exclusive wellness blueprint—a healing path crafted only for you.

*   This is not a pre-set package; it is your personal prescription for deep and lasting health.

*   Your plan will detail your bespoke schedule of therapies and any necessary *Panchakarma* procedures.

*   It also includes your tailored diet, in-house herbal medicines, and specific lifestyle guidance.

**Daily Healing with Expert Care**

This is where your deep healing truly begins, with daily therapies delivered with skill and compassionate, expert oversight.

*   You will receive your prescribed treatments each day from our highly skilled and caring therapists.

*   All therapies are performed using our own authentic, high-quality herbal oils and preparations.

*   Your progress is closely monitored by our physicians to ensure your journey is both safe and effective.

**Holistic Lifestyle Integration**

We believe true healing extends beyond the treatment room, nurturing you with wholesome food and mindful practices.

*   Enjoy three fresh, *sattvic* vegetarian meals daily, prepared from our own organic farm produce.

*   Harmonize your mind and body in our expansive 6000 sq ft yoga center with daily sessions.

*   Immerse yourself in enriching activities like cultural performances and Ayurvedic cooking classes.

**Empowerment for Your Journey Home**

Our support for you doesn’t end when you leave; we empower you with the tools for a lifetime of wellness.

*   Before you depart, you will receive a comprehensive final consultation with your physician.

*   We will provide you with a detailed plan, including dietary and lifestyle advice, to continue your progress at home.

*   Our global network of centers is always available to provide you with ongoing support.

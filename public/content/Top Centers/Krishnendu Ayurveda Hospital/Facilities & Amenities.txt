### Facilities & Amenities

At Krishnendu, every facility has been thoughtfully designed to support your healing journey in an environment of comfort and authenticity.

**Comfortable Accommodation Options**

Rest and recover in a space designed for your comfort, privacy, and healing.

*   Choose from a range of options, from budget-friendly rooms to premium poolside cottages.

*   All rooms feature modern ensuite bathrooms, comfortable beds, and daily housekeeping.

*   Enjoy a clean, peaceful, and well-ventilated space with plenty of natural light.

**Authentic Treatment Infrastructure**

Experience the best of traditional healing, supported by modern standards of hygiene and safety.

*   Heal in comfort in our dedicated therapy halls, designed for authentic Ayurvedic procedures.

*   Benefit from the purity of our own in-house pharmacy, where your medicines are freshly prepared.

*   Trust in our care, with NABH-compliant hygiene and sterilization protocols in place.

**Yoga & Meditation Hall**

Harmonize your mind and body in our serene spaces dedicated to mindful and therapeutic practice.

*   Find your inner peace in our spacious and calming hall, perfect for daily practice.

*   Our therapeutic yoga sessions are designed to complement your medical treatments.

*   Learn powerful pranayama and meditation techniques to calm your mind and accelerate healing.

**Swimming Pool & Hydrotherapy**

Enjoy the therapeutic benefits of water-based exercises in our well-maintained swimming pool.

*   Our clean, beautiful pool is perfect for gentle, low-impact exercise and relaxation.

*   Hydrotherapy is especially beneficial for those with joint pain, arthritis, or mobility issues.

*   Relax and unwind on comfortable poolside loungers between your therapy sessions.

**Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Center**

Accelerate your recovery with our state-of-the-art physiotherapy and rehabilitation unit.

*   Our expert physiotherapy team works in synergy with your Ayurvedic treatments for faster results.

*   This is a crucial part of our integrative approach to spine care, joint pain, and post-stroke recovery.

*   Benefit from modern rehabilitation techniques that help restore your mobility and strength.

**Multi-Gym & Fitness Center**

Maintain your fitness goals in our well-equipped, modern gym facility.

*   Our fitness center is available for supervised exercise programs.

*   It is particularly valuable for our weight management and general fitness programs.

*   Our trained instructors can guide you through a safe and effective workout.

**Herbal Garden & Medicinal Plants**

Deepen your connection to your healing by walking through the source of our medicines.

*   Our beautiful organic herbal garden is home to many of the potent plants used in your treatments.

*   Enjoy educational walks with our doctors, who will explain the healing properties of the herbs.

*   This garden is a living testament to our commitment to fresh, pure, and authentic formulations.

**Traditional Vegetarian Restaurant**

Savor delicious meals where food is treated as the first and most powerful medicine.

*   Enjoy simple, wholesome, and delicious vegetarian meals prepared according to classical Ayurvedic principles.

*   Your meals are customized by our physicians to support your specific treatment plan and balance your *doshas*.

*   Experience the authentic taste of Kerala's therapeutic, *sattvic* cuisine.

**Professional Consultation Rooms**

A private and comfortable space for deep, personal conversations with your physician.

*   This is where your unique healing journey is crafted through a deep, personal connection.

*   Experience traditional diagnostics like pulse reading (*Nadi Pariksha*) with our skilled doctors.

*   Benefit from our approach of regular doctor visits to monitor your progress.

**24/7 Medical Support & Safety**

Feel completely secure with our professional medical support and internationally recognized safety standards.

*   Enjoy complete peace of mind with our Ayurvedic physicians available on call 24/7.

*   Our NABH accreditation ensures that every aspect of your care meets rigorous safety protocols.

*   We have established emergency procedures for your complete safety and peace of mind.

**Hassle-Free Guest Services**

We handle all the practical details so you can focus completely on your healing journey.

*   Allow us to arrange your airport transfers for a seamless and stress-free arrival.

*   Enjoy the convenience of our daily housekeeping and in-house laundry services.

*   Our team can assist with travel arrangements for family members wishing to explore local attractions.

**Educational & Engagement Programs**

We believe that an informed patient is an empowered patient, and we make you an active partner in your healing.

*   Deepen your understanding of your health through special classes on Ayurvedic principles.

### Kairali Heritage: A Riverside Ayurveda Sanctuary in Kannur

Discover a tranquil haven of healing at Kairali Heritage, a serene wellness resort nestled on the picturesque banks of the Kattampally River in Kannur. Surrounded by 11 acres of lush mangrove forests, this retreat offers a perfect blend of authentic Ayurvedic healing and the warm, heartfelt hospitality of North Kerala. Here, your wellness journey unfolds in a peaceful, pollution-free atmosphere where you can truly reconnect with nature and your inner self.

### The Kairali Heritage Experience

Our resort is thoughtfully designed to provide a complete and comfortable healing experience.

**Riverside Living**
*   Stay in one of our 24 air-conditioned cottages, each offering serene, calming views of the river.
*   Enjoy modern amenities seamlessly blended with traditional architecture for a comfortable and gracious stay.
*   Immerse yourself in a nature-friendly atmosphere, away from the chaos of city life.

**A Land of Natural Abundance**
*   Kairali Heritage is located in Kannur, a district of Kerala blessed with pristine beaches, majestic hills, and tranquil backwaters.
*   Our location is steeped in the rich traditional art and culture of "God's Own Country."
*   Enjoy easy accessibility, with Kannur International Airport and the railway station just a short drive away.

**How to Reach**

**By Air:** Nearest airport is Kannur International Airport (16 km), India's fourth largest airport. Alternative airports include Calicut Airport (115 km south) and Mangalore Airport (170 km north). Airport taxi services and resort transportation available on request.

**By Train:** Kannur Railway Station is the nearest railhead located 12 km from the resort. Major trains from Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, and other cities stop at Kannur. The resort can arrange pickup services from the station.

**By Road:** Kairali Heritage is located on the Parassini Kadavu route, easily accessible by road from Kannur city and surrounding areas. The resort is well-connected via National Highway network. Private vehicles, taxis, and auto-rickshaws readily available.


**Transportation Services**

Pick-up and drop services available on request for guests arriving from Kannur Railway Station, Kannur Airport, or nearby locations. Advance booking and arrival details required for seamless transportation arrangements. Travel desk assists with local sightseeing tours and transportation coordination.


**Customer Support**

You are only a phone call away from Kairali Heritage customer support. The front desk operates 24/7 to assist with reservations, queries, special requests, and emergency support ensuring comfortable and memorable guest experiences.

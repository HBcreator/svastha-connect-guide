### Frequently Asked Questions About Kairali Heritage

**1. What is the minimum duration for wellness programs at Kairali Heritage?**

Wellness programs can range from weekend getaways (2-3 days) to comprehensive week-long retreats and extended Panchakarma programs (14-21 days). Minimum stay duration depends on chosen wellness package and individual health goals. Even brief weekend stays provide relaxation benefits, while longer durations offer deeper therapeutic results and sustained health improvements.

**2. How far is Kairali Heritage from Kannur city and airport?**

Kairali Heritage is conveniently located just 12 kilometers (6 miles) from Kannur railway station and 16 kilometers from Kannur International Airport, making it easily accessible for both domestic and international travelers. Alternative airports include Calicut Airport (115 km south) and Mangalore Airport (170 km north), providing multiple convenient travel options for guests.

**3. Do I need prior experience with Ayurveda to benefit from treatments?**

No prior experience is necessary. Kairali Heritage welcomes complete beginners and provides comprehensive consultations where qualified Ayurvedic physicians explain treatments, answer questions, and design gentle introduction programs. The experienced medical team personalizes all protocols according to individual comfort levels, health conditions, and familiarity with Ayurvedic practices.

**4. What type of food is served at Kairali Heritage?**

The multi-cuisine restaurant serves delicious Kerala cuisine featuring local specialties, North Indian dishes, seafood preparations, and vegetarian options. For wellness program guests, therapeutic Ayurvedic vegetarian meals are prepared using fresh local ingredients according to individual dietary requirements, dosha constitutions, and health conditions as prescribed by Ayurvedic physicians.

**5. Is Kairali Heritage suitable for family vacations?**

Absolutely. Kairali Heritage is an ideal family destination with spacious rooms accommodating families, outdoor swimming pool with separate children's area, recreational facilities including indoor games, houseboat rides on Kattampally River, ample walking spaces, safe peaceful environment, and multiple dining options catering to various age groups and preferences.

**6. Can guests explore nearby tourist attractions from the resort?**

Yes, Kairali Heritage's travel desk assists with arranging tours to nearby attractions including Payambalam Beach, St. Angelo Fort, Parassinikadavu Temple, Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach, Ezhimala Beach, traditional Theyyam performances, and other Kannur district attractions. Transportation can be arranged through the resort for convenient sightseeing while maintaining wellness treatment schedules.

**7. What should I pack for my wellness stay at Kairali Heritage?**

Comfortable loose cotton clothing suitable for treatments and Kerala climate, personal toiletries, any regular medications, previous medical reports if applicable, swimwear for pool, sunscreen, insect repellent, walking shoes for exploring the 11-acre property, and an open mind for healing. The resort provides treatment gowns, towels, and necessary wellness amenities.

**8. Are houseboat rides available and what is the experience like?**

Yes, Kairali Heritage offers memorable houseboat cruises on the scenic Kattampally River surrounded by mangrove forests and picturesque village landscapes. The houseboat experience includes freshly prepared onboard meals featuring Kerala cuisine and seafood specialties, opportunities for bird watching, observing local fishing activities, and enjoying serene river views. Advance booking recommended with additional charges applicable.

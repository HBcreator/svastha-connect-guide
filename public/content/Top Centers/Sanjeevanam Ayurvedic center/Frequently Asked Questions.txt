### Frequently Asked Questions

**1. What is the minimum recommended stay?**

For our wellness programs, we recommend a minimum of 7 days. For our medical treatment programs, a stay of 14 days or more is typically required for deep, effective, and measurable healing, as advised by our integrative team of physicians.

**2. What is different about Sanjeevanam compared to a traditional Ayurvedic retreat?**

Sanjeevanam is a modern, NABH-accredited integrative hospital, not a resort. Our unique strength lies in our multi-disciplinary approach, where we blend the diagnostic precision of modern medicine with the deep, holistic healing of Ayurveda and the functional restoration of physiotherapy, all under one roof.

**3. What kind of food will I be served?**

You will be served a delicious and wholesome vegetarian diet. Our approach to nutrition is integrative, where our expert dieticians and Ayurvedic chefs collaborate to create a personalized meal plan for you that is both scientifically balanced and aligned with classical Ayurvedic principles.

**4. Should I bring my current medications and medical records?**

Yes, absolutely. It is crucial that you bring all your current medications and recent medical records. Our integrative team of both Ayurvedic and modern medicine doctors will review them to create a safe and comprehensive treatment plan for you.

**5. How do I get to the hospital?**

We are conveniently located in the heart of Kochi, Kerala, making us easily accessible. We are approximately a one-hour drive from the Cochin International Airport (COK). We are happy to help you coordinate your airport transfers for a seamless journey.

**6. How is my treatment plan personalized?**

Your personalization process is deeply scientific. It begins with an integrative diagnosis, using both modern lab tests and traditional Ayurvedic methods like pulse reading. Based on this complete, evidence-based picture of your health, our multi-disciplinary team collaborates to craft your unique healing blueprint.

**7. Can a family member or companion stay with me?**

Yes, companions are welcome to stay with you in our comfortable, well-appointed rooms. They can enjoy our healthy cuisine and the peaceful environment of our hospital. Please note that treatments are only provided to those with a prescribed wellness plan.

**8. Is this a suitable place for post-surgery rehabilitation?**

Yes, our integrative approach is ideal for post-surgery rehabilitation. We create a powerful synergy where our Ayurvedic treatments help to reduce inflammation and accelerate tissue healing, while our expert physiotherapy team works to restore your strength, mobility, and function.

**9. What should I pack for my stay?**

We recommend packing light, comfortable cotton clothing. Please prioritize bringing all your medical records and current medications. We will provide towels, basic toiletries, and any necessary attire for your treatments.

**10. Is there internet or Wi-Fi available?**

Yes, Wi-Fi is available in our facility. However, for a deeper healing experience, we gently encourage our guests to minimize screen time and embrace the opportunity to rest their minds and connect with their inner healing process.

### Insurance & Payment Info

Making your path to integrative healing accessible with flexible payment and professional insurance support.

**Insurance Coverage**

*   As a NABH-accredited hospital, many of our medical treatments are eligible for coverage by Indian health insurance.

*   We provide a cashless treatment facility for eligible conditions to ensure your stay is seamless and stress-free.

*   Our professional administrative team provides all necessary medical documentation to support your claims process.

*   We advise you to confirm coverage details directly with your insurance provider before your stay begins.

**Payment Options**

*   We accept all major payment methods, including credit/debit cards, UPI, and domestic/international bank transfers.

*   Our pricing is completely transparent, with all costs clearly outlined in a personalized quotation before your stay.

*   Flexible payment options and special pricing are available for corporate and group wellness programs.

*   Please inquire about any seasonal offers or special package rates when you make your booking.

**For International Patients**

International guests are encouraged to check with their home insurance providers regarding coverage for our integrative and Ayurvedic treatments. Our administrative team is experienced in providing the necessary medical reports and documentation in the required format to support your international insurance claims.

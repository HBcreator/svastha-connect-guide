### Insurance & Payment Info

Making your path to pure healing simple, accessible, and transparent.

**Insurance Coverage**

*   As a NABH-accredited facility, many of our medical treatments are eligible for insurance claims in India.

*   Our professional administrative team provides all necessary medical documentation to support your claims process.

*   We are committed to helping you navigate the reimbursement process as smoothly as possible.

*   We strongly advise you to confirm coverage details directly with your insurance provider before your stay.

**Payment Options**

*   We accept all major payment methods, including credit/debit cards and domestic/international bank transfers.

*   Our pricing is completely transparent, with all costs clearly outlined in a personalized quotation after your consultation.

*   Our focus on "healing over frills" ensures that our pricing offers exceptional value for authentic, high-quality care.

*   You will receive a detailed cost estimate before your treatment begins, with absolutely no hidden charges.

**For International Patients**

International guests are encouraged to check with their home insurance providers regarding coverage for Ayurvedic treatments. Our administrative team is experienced in providing the necessary medical reports and documentation in the required format to support your international insurance claims.
